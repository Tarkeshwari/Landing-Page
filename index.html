About cosmetics

Cosmetics are a class of health and cosmetic goods intended to improve a person's physical appearance. Cosmetics are items created to beautify, protect, and modify the look of our bodies' exterior features. Cosmetics are made up of mixes of chemical components that are either obtained from natural sources or generated synthetically. Cosmetics serve a variety of functions. The body or skin can be cleansed or protected using those intended for personal care and skin care.
